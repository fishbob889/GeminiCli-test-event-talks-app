Finance News Headlines - January 7, 2026

Australian Market:
- The ASX 200 was trading approximately 0.5% higher after a solid inflation print for November.
- Headline inflation in Australia was flat month-on-month and up 3.4% year-on-year, slightly below forecasts.
- Trimmed mean inflation was up 0.3% month-on-month and 3.2% year-on-year.
- Bluescope shares rallied 20.8% after receiving a non-binding indicative offer from a consortium to acquire the company at $30.00 a piece.
- The One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA) lifted its 2026 fiscal outlook, with tax relief and capital expenditure incentives supporting consumption and growth.
